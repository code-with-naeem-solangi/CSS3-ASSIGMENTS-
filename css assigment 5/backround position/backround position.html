Magnolia

Magnolia grandiflora. commonly known as the southern magnolia or bull bay , is a tree of the family Magnoliaceae native to the southeastern United States. from coastal virginia south to central Florida, and west to eastern Texas and Oklahoma.Reaching 27.5 m (90 fit) in height. it is a large striking evergreen tree with large dark green leaves and large white fragrant flowers. Widely cultivated aroundthe world over a hundred cultivars have been bred and marketed commercially.
